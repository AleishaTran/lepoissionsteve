Project KANE uses NeRF-based techniques to help mobile robots perceive and interact with complex environments in 3D.

Abstract

In this project, we explore the integration of Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF) with mobile robotic platforms for the purpose of spatial understanding and visualisation. Our system reconstructs scenes using RGB-D data to generate high-fidelity 3D models in dynamic environments. These reconstructions enhance robot perception, enabling better navigation, interaction, and user guidance in complex spaces such as art galleries or research labs.
